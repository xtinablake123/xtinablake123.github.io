Welcome to Michael Bay's Daycare Service

At Michael Bay's Daycare Service your child will be "taken care of" by the defenders of the universe. You will get to choose which transformer "takes care of" your child based upon his/her needs. We offer many services, but one thing we will guarantee is your child will get to do things you, the parent, would never let him/her do. The use of granades, guns, knives, and dynamite jets are done almost daily. The skills of flying fighter jets, semi-trucks, and sports cars are also a key component of our daycare. Think of what your child will be able to do with the skills we teach!

Thank you for visiting our site. We hope you will consider chaos, explosives, and giant transformers to take care of your child while you're away!
